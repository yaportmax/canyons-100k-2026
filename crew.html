§ 04 — PRE-RACEFriday & race morning.

Friday PM

Pack the car.

  1. One labeled kit per crew station (Michigan Bluff, Foresthill, Driver's Flat, Finish)
  2. Cooler with ice — Tailwind premix, Coke, pickles, iced coffee
  3. Every restock comes from crew only.
  4. Chair, shade, blanket, headlamps (×2), extra batteries
  5. Full tank of gas. Foresthill has no 24h pump.
  6. Download offline maps for Auburn, Foresthill, Mosquito Ridge, Driver's Flat Rd
Friday night

Coordinate.

  • Confirm the runner's bib number — it's their tracker ID on live results
  • Bookmark the UTMB live-tracking page on every phone
  • Agree on a text check-in after every aid station
  • Runner in bed by 20:00. Set three alarms.
  • Crew meet-up time: 03:30 at the Auburn finish lot, or wherever you're staying
  • Runner eats breakfast ~03:15 (2h before start). Coffee, toast, banana, honey.
Saturday 04:00

Race morning.

  1. Shuttle to China Wall leaves from Auburn — check exact time in runner guide
  2. Start is 05:00. Crew does not drive to China Wall — head directly to Michigan Bluff via Foresthill Rd
  3. Plan to arrive Michigan Bluff early enough to park, walk in, and be ready before the runner arrives.
  4. Keep the runner's phone charging in the car. Weigh-in bag in the car.
  5. Text the runner's family a live-tracking link once the race starts
